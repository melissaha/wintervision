Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best route to take to Winter Vision?


For directions to Koinonia Conference Campgrounds, please click here.

Note: DO NOT take the Summit Road route. Your GPS may advise you to go this way, but this road is very narrow and closes unexpectedly. See the recommended directions at the page linked above.


I'm a student from Southern California. Is transporation available?


Consider taking the bus! There are only 50 spots and it often fills up very quickly. In the rare case that there are bus cancellations, we do keep a wait list but we recommend registering for Winter Vision and the bus ASAP. Round-Trip bus ride is $50. One-Way bus ride is $33. More details can be found on the registration form.

Can I visit my students or friends at Winter Vision?


Sorry, there are no visitation hours during Winter Vision. With such a short weekend, we want all students and staff to be fully immersed in their experience at camp.


I've registered, sent in the release forms, and paid for camp -- does that mean I'm confirmed for Winter Vision?


No, not until you receive a separate email confirming your registration for camp. Camp attendance has grown over the last few years and we want to take the time to comfirm each registration on an individual basis. Please be patient with us as we go through registration confirmations as this is a manual process! In the rare event that you are turned down after submitting payment, we will return your payment in full. Thank you for understanding.


What is the refund or cancellation policy?


We know that cancellations may happen unexpectedly due to unforseen situations. However, we must confirm and put a down payment for every camper's registration with Koinonia by a certain date. Below is the cancellation/refund policies:

Camp Refunds
Cancellations will not be refunded after Jan 11, 2015. No-shows will not be refunded. All cancellations before Jan 11, 2015 will be charged a processing fee of $25.

Bus Refunds
Bus cancellations will not be refunded after Dec 31, 2014. No-shows will not be refunded. All cancellations before Dec 31, 2014 will be charged a processing fee of $10.
