Camp Theme

We live in difficult times. The sins of racism, corruption, deception, and selfishness have always existed but are even more evident in recent events. Where do we find true hope in a world desperate for hope? Lamentations 3:22-24 reminds us that the Lord is our portion--He is all we have. At Winter Vision, we will all be reminded that even if everything we value is taken away, we find all we need in Jesus. In our hearts and with our mouths, we will all declare, “Jesus is all I have.”

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Winter Vision and who is it for?

Winter Vision is a youth camp (students 6th - 12th grade) started by the OMS Holiness Church of North America. Although we sometimes allow students from other churches outside of the Conference to register, we prioritize student registrations from within the Conference.


I've registered and paid for camp -- does that mean my student is confirmed for Winter Vision?

No, not until you receive a separate email from the Registrar confirming your student's registration for camp. Camp attendance has grown over the last few years and we want to take the time to comfirm each registration on an individual basis. Please be patient with us as this is a manual process! In the rare event that you are turned down after submitting payment, we will return your payment in full. Thank you for understanding.


My student is from Northern California. Is transporation available?

Unfortunately, Winter Vision does not organize a bus or carpools from Northern California. We encourage you to speak with your church's youth staff, pastor(s), or other parents to coordinate carpools.


My student is from Southern California. Is transporation available?

Consider taking the bus! There are only 54 spots and it often fills up very quickly. In the rare case that there are bus cancellations, we do keep a wait list but we recommend registering for Winter Vision and the bus ASAP.

Round-Trip bus ride is $60. One-Way bus ride is $33. More details can be found on the registration form.
